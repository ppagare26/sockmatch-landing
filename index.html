SockMatch AI

SockMatch AI is an intelligent fashion assistant that helps sneaker lovers find the perfect socks to match their sneakers. It analyzes the sneaker’s design, color, and height from uploaded images or text input, and then recommends stylish, season-appropriate socks.

How It Works

  • Users upload a sneaker image or enter sneaker name & colorway
  • The AI extracts design elements and color patterns
  • Based on matching logic and rules, it recommends socks by type, color, pattern, and material
  • It then shows affiliate links for users to shop directly from Amazon

Why Amazon

We plan to use the Amazon Product Advertising API to fetch real-time listings of socks that match the AI’s recommendation. This gives users immediate purchase options while we earn affiliate commissions through the Amazon Associates program.

Current Status

The tool is currently under development and will be deployed soon as a web-based platform. This page serves as a preview of our purpose and implementation plan for API access.
